About AVM

The Alpine Verification Meeting (AVM) is an informal, annual workshop series dedicated to formal verification. It brings together PhD students and researchers primarily from the Alpine region and surrounding countries to discuss current challenges and advances in the field.

Call for Participation

We invite researchers and PhD students working in formal verification and related areas to participate in AVM'26. If you would like to give a talk, please submit a tentative title and abstract to the organizers by August 2026 (currently not yet open).
